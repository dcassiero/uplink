Doom

Welcome to DOOM, a lightning-fast virtual reality adventure where you're the toughest space trooper ever to suck vacuum. Your mission is to shoot your way through a monster-infested holocaust. Living to tell the tale if possible.

The Doom Menu

To access the menu from the intro, press the SPACEBAR. When you are in the game, press the ESC key to access the menu. When in the menu, use the arrow keys to select an option, and press ENTER to activate the selection. Use the BACKSPACE key to return to the previous menu item.

Playing the Game

Doom provides on-screen information in the status bar that's necessary to survive. Messages will appear on the game screen when you run over various bonus items and artifacts. WALKING: Use the up and down arrow keys to walk forward and backward. TURNING: The left and right arrow keys turn you left and right. RUNNING: Hold down the right Shift key and the appropriate arrow key to speed yourself up. STRAFE: By holding down the Alt key and the right or left arrow key, you can sidestep, rather than turn left or right.
