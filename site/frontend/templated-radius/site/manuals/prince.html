Prince of Persia

Rescue the Princess from the Grand Vizier Jaffar!

Running, Jumping, Climbing

Use the arrow keys to move left, right up or down. Alternatively, use the NUM keys to move.

To turn around: tap left or right

To run: hold down left or right

To take careful steps: hold down SHIFT and either left or right

To jump up: hold the up key

To jump forward: use the 7 (up left) or 9 (up right) keys

To climb up a ledge: stand near the ledge and press up

To climb down a ledge: Stand near the ledge, turn around and press the down key

To duck: press the down key

To pick up something: stand in front of the item and press SHIFT

Sword Fighting

To strike: SHIFT

To advance: either left or right keys, depending which direction your opponent is in

To retreat: opposite of advance

Blocking: hold up as soon as your opponent strikes, timing is key!

Stop fighting: hit the down key, this puts your sword away
